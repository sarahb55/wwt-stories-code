After discussions with colleagues, the collaborators realize that the “zero” of Galactic Latitude as it’s defined by the International Astronomical Union (IAU) does not mark the Galactic Plane on the Sky, because the Sun is slightly above the plane, and the Galactic center is a bit off from the Galactic origin defined in 1959. They wonder where the plane should be on the Sky and whether Nessie is close to it...
